My Story

I am a Test Engineer at Glucode, primarily focused on writing and maintain UI tests as well as providing adequate quality assurance on new and existing features. My role is focused on mobile applications, specifically iOS and the accompanying suite of applications. I enjoy testing my limits and embrace challenges, completing tasks to the highest possible standard. Ilike to think of myself as a fast and eager learner. IT has always been a part of me as a person, from avery young age I knew that I would study IT.I’ve been working towards gaining experience in not only Quality Assurance, but also sharpeningskills like project management as I work closely with business whenever there is a new future or abig project. Working closely with scrum masters, I have also gained working experience using theAgile methodology, this is particularly effective in my current working environment where we mustadapt to changes at a fast pace. I also pride myself in debugging alongside the developers to gain amore in-depth understanding of the application and to try and avoid similar defects in the future.
